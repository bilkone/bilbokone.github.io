When the gloomy cold weather months hit, they hit hard. Your first reaction? To beeline it to the Bahamas for a winter vacation. Immediately. Or any other place where you can sip on some skinny margs and show off that bikini bod you've worked so hard to maintain over the past few months. But as great as these exotic getaways are, we'd like to propose an alternative to the go-to winter escape: Embrace the season's chilly temps. Donning a heavy, clunky coat and taking advantage of the arctic chill can lead to a slew of health and mood benefits. (Plus, they can be just as fun!) Done right, this year's real winter vacation will have you wishing it was snowy year-round.
